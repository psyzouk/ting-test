About Ting

A multicultural lens, grounded in lived experience.

draft — replace with Ting's approved wording

Ting Lee is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor (New York) and Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor (California) who has spent roughly the last nine years walking alongside people through trauma, transition, and the search for belonging. Having moved through the world as an international student and immigrant herself, she understands first-hand how identity, culture, and migration shape the way we feel and heal.

Her work centers people who are too often underserved — international students, immigrants, and anyone navigating questions of racial identity — in a warm, unhurried space where teens, adults, couples, and families can make sense of what they're carrying and move toward steadier ground.
